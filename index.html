My Favorite Food: Momo

Momo is my favorite dish because it's incredibly flavorful and comforting. The soft, steamed dumplings filled with succulent meat or vegetables are simply irresistible. Whether served with a spicy chutney or a light soup, momo never fails to satisfy my cravings.

How to Make Momo

  1. Prepare the dough.
  2. Make the filling:
    1. Finely chop your choice of meat or vegetables.
    2. Mix with spices like ginger, garlic, and chili.
  3. Fill the momo wrappers with the filling.
  4. Steam the momos for about 10-15 minutes.
  5. Serve with chutney or soup.

Side Dishes to Have with Momo

  • Fried Rice
  • Soup
  • Salad
    • Tomatoes
    • Cucumber
